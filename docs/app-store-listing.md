```
DiscVault is a privacy-first collection manager for your physical movies and series — 4K UHD, Blu-ray, and DVD. Point your camera at a barcode and DiscVault fills in the title, year, cover art, and format for you. Everything lives on your device.

BUILT FOR REAL SHELVES
• Scan a disc's barcode to add it in seconds
• Automatic title, year, cover art, and format lookup
• Add titles manually by searching for a name
• Track box sets and disc formats
• Keep a personal watchlist and watch history

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
• No account required — just install and start scanning
• No analytics, no advertising, no third-party tracking
• Your collection is stored on your device, not on our servers
• The only online lookup sends just a barcode or search term — never your identity, your library, or your contacts

WORKS THE WAY COLLECTORS DO
• Fast, clean library that stays snappy on large collections
• Filter and search by title and format
• Camera scanning runs on-device with Apple's built-in scanner

DiscVault is part of the wider self-hosted DiscVault project for collectors who want to keep their physical media catalogue under their own control. Connecting the app to your own self-hosted DiscVault server is coming in a future update.

Questions or feedback? privacy@discvault.eu
```

### App Review notes
```
No account or login is required to review this app.

HOW TO TEST
• Grant camera access and scan any retail disc barcode (Blu-ray/DVD/4K), or
• Tap add and search for a title by name to add it manually.

CAMERA: used only to scan a disc barcode, on-device via VisionKit. Camera
images are never stored or transmitted; only the decoded barcode number is sent
for a metadata lookup.

NETWORK: the app contacts one HTTPS service, MovieVault
(https://movies.vaultstack.eu), which is operated by us (DiscVault — VaultStack)
and hosted in the EU. It receives only the scanned barcode or typed search term,
plus a randomly generated device identifier used to issue a token and rate-limit
abuse. No name, email, Apple ID, location, or contacts are sent.

PRIVACY LABEL: Device ID and Search History are collected for App Functionality
only — not linked to identity, not used for tracking. No ATT prompt is required
(the app does no tracking). Full details:
https://discvault.eu/privacy.html

Server sync is not enabled in this release.
```
